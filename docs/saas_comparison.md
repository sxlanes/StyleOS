## 2. Strategic Advantages of StyleOS

### A. Brand Sovereignty (The "Shopify" Model)
Unlike Planity, where you are one row in a list of 100, StyleOS gives you a **dedicated, premium digital home**.
*   **Actionable Advice**: We avoid the "Directory Trap". StyleOS salons feel like Apple Stores, not Amazon listings.

### B. Sarah IA: Operation-Led Growth
Sarah is not just a chatbot; she's an operational employee.
*   **Comparison**: Planity relies on users doing the work. StyleOS relies on the machine doing the work.

### C. The "Blue-Chip" Financial Model
By charging a flat fee instead of commissions, StyleOS aligns with the salon owner's success.
*   **High-Margin Impact**: As the salon grows, StyleOS becomes cheaper relative to revenue, whereas Planity becomes more expensive.
